Cookie Settings

Good Timing uses cookies to operate the site and improve your experience. This page explains what cookies we use and how to manage or remove them in your browser. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Cookies We Use

We use four categories of cookies:

Essential

Required for the site to function — for example, keeping you logged in. These cannot be disabled without breaking core features.

Functional

Remember your preferences and settings (such as language or region) so you don't have to re-enter them each visit.

Performance & Analytics

Help us understand how visitors use the site — which pages are visited, how long people stay, and where errors occur. We use Google Analytics for this. See the opt-out option below.

Retargeting & Advertising

Used to show relevant advertisements based on your browsing activity. These are set by our advertising partners.

Manage Cookies in Your Browser

The simplest way to control cookies is through your browser settings. Use the links below to go directly to the cookie and privacy settings for your browser:

Chrome Safari Firefox Edge

Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Services — some features may not work correctly if essential or functional cookies are blocked.

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal that tells websites you'd prefer not to be tracked. Our Services do not currently respond to Do Not Track signals. To learn more about Do Not Track, visit www.allaboutdnt.com.
